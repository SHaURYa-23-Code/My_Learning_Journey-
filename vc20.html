Typographic Pseudo-Elements Demo

1. Editorial Formatting (::first-letter & ::first-line)

Typography is the art and technique of arranging type to make written language legible, readable, and appealing when displayed. The arrangement of type involves selecting typefaces, point sizes, line lengths, line-spacing, and letter-spacing, and adjusting the space between pairs of letters. Try resizing your browser window to see how the colored text dynamically adapts to whatever fits on the very first line.

2. Interactive Text Selection (::selection)

Highlight this specific text paragraph with your mouse cursor or touch gesture. Instead of the standard browser default blue highlight color layout, it will use a custom branded color theme defined directly in our CSS file.
